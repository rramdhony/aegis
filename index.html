FAIR Guidelines

Mauritius has published its national AI governance framework. Most organisations are not yet aligned.

The FAIR Guidelines, published by the Ministry of Information Technology, Communication and Innovation, establish clear expectations for how organisations in Mauritius develop, deploy, and oversee AI systems.

For regulated entities, government bodies, and private organisations where AI is already shaping decisions, alignment is not optional. It is a governance obligation that carries real accountability.

Aegis offers FAIR gap assessments, training, and implementation support. We help your organisation move from awareness to alignment, with a structured approach and no pressure to buy beyond what the work requires.
